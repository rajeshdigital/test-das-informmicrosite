3. Funding, if you pay the levy

From 1 May 2017, if you're an employer with a payroll of more than £3 million, the way you access funding for apprenticeships will change.

Find out about the apprenticeship levy in more detail.

You'll be able to access your apprenticeship funding through the digital apprenticeship service where you can:

  • create or sign in to your account
  • link PAYE schemes to your account
  • authorise payments to your training provider

Find out more about how to set up and manage your digital apprenticeship account.

In your account you can also:

  • estimate your apprenticeship funding to help calculate how much you’ll have to spend on apprenticeship training
  • choose apprenticeship training for your business and then find a training provider
  • work with your training provider to advertise an apprenticeship
